Join Us for the Ties and Tiaras Ball 2025!

Get ready for an afternoon filled with enchantment, laughter, and unforgettable memories! The beloved Ties and Tiaras Ball returns on Saturday, April 26, 2025, from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM at the Willow Ridge Community Centre in Calgary. This family-friendly event invites parents and children alike to don their royal best and join in the fun.

Whether you dream of twirling on the dance floor, transforming into your favourite character with stunning face painting by Face Art Calgary, or unleashing your creativity at the crown-decorating craft station, there’s something magical for everyone. Young adventurers can leap and laugh in the bouncy castle, while families enjoy interactive games designed to delight guests of all ages.

Highlights of the Day

  • Dancing – Let loose on the dance floor and show off your best moves.
  • Face Painting – Transform into your favourite character with designs by Face Art Calgary.
  • Craft Station – Create a unique, personalized paper princess crown.
  • Bouncy Castle – Jump and play for endless fun.
  • Cupcakes – Treat yourself to stunning creations by Cakes by Freya (available for purchase).
  • Games – Enjoy engaging games designed for all ages.

Don’t forget to satisfy your sweet tooth with beautifully designed cupcakes available for purchase from Cakes by Freya.

Dress Up and Join the Fun

Costumes are encouraged! Children can come dressed as their favourite characters, while adults can wear themed accessories to get into the royal spirit.
