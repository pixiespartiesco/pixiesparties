Welcome to Pixies Parties: Where Calgary’s Magical Moments Begin!

At Pixies Parties, we specialize in creating unforgettable, character-driven experiences for kids' parties throughout Calgary and surrounding areas. From enchanting princesses to brave superheroes and whimsical fairies, we bring stories to life and spark joy in the hearts of little ones. Our team of trained entertainers knows just how to blend fun and fantasy, making every celebration a true fairy-tale experience.

Meet Erin Mackey, Founder of Pixies Parties

I’m Erin Mackey, the proud owner and main magic maker at Pixies Parties. From as early as I can remember, I loved playing dress-up and getting lost in the world of make-believe. That passion for creativity and storytelling naturally evolved into Pixies Parties. It’s more than just a job—it’s my way of spreading joy, laughter, and a bit of magic. I’ve always believed that a sprinkle of imagination can turn an ordinary day into an extraordinary adventure. With four years of hands-on experience in children’s party entertainment, I’ve brought characters like Elsa, Rapunzel, and Spider-Gwen to life at countless events. In 2023, I turned my passion into Pixies Parties, offering a unique blend of immersive storytelling, engaging activities, and character interactions tailored to make every party special for Calgary families.

Why Pixies Parties Stands Out in Calgary’s Party Scene

Authentic Character Performances: Our talented cast embodies the magic of your child’s favourite characters, from beloved princesses like Cinderella and Ariel to adventurous heroines like Hermione and Spider-Gwen. We pride ourselves on providing not just costumes but performances that captivate and inspire imagination.

Locally Owned and Community-Focused: We’re based right here in Calgary and committed to giving back to our community. Giving back is an essential part of our mission at Pixies Parties. I actively volunteer with Autism Calgary, offering inclusive entertainment for children with diverse needs. Our characters are trained to engage with kids of all abilities, ensuring that every child feels included, valued, and celebrated. It’s about bringing a little magic to every child’s life, no matter their background or circumstances.

Customizable Party Packages for Every Occasion: Whether it’s a small backyard gathering or a large birthday bash, we offer a variety of packages that fit your budget and event size. From themed games and sing-alongs to interactive storytelling, we ensure your party is packed with memorable moments.
