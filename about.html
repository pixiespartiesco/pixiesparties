About

Welcome to the whimsical world of Pixies Parties! Founded by Erin Mackey in August 2023, nestled in the heart of Calgary, AB. With a sprinkle of pixie dust and a dash of imagination, we specialize in crafting extraordinary children's party entertainment experiences that linger in the memory.
At Pixies Parties, we believe in turning dreams into reality. Our cast of characters includes beloved princesses, mischievous pirates, whimsical witches, and more. Each performer is dedicated to bringing the magic alive, with costumes so authentic and personalities so enchanting, your guests will be spellbound.
With a passion for creating joy and over 3 years of personal experience in the children's entertainment industry, founder Erin Mackey envisioned a space where kids could interact with their favorite characters up close. Pixies Parties was born from that dream, making every child's special day a fairy tale come true.
We understand that children's parties are more than just events – they're cherished memories in the making. Our team goes above and beyond to ensure that every child is at the center of attention, creating interactive and immersive experiences that captivate their imagination and create lasting smiles.
Pixies Parties takes pride in being more than just an entertainment company. We're memory-makers, happiness-spreaders, and dream-weavers. From story time with princesses to swashbuckling adventures with pirates, our performers engage, entertain, and create an atmosphere of pure magic.
While we call Calgary our home, our magical experiences are only limited by the range of our magic pumpkin carriage. We love to travel, spreading our unique brand of enchantment to neighboring towns and cities. Wherever your event may be, Pixies Parties is ready to bring the sparkle and smiles.
Whether it's a birthday party, a special celebration, or any occasion deserving of magic, Pixies Parties is here to make it extraordinary. Let your child's eyes light up as they meet their favorite characters in person. It's not just a party – it's a journey into a world of wonder and joy.
