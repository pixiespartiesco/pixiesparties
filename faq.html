Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of children's parties do you specialize in?

    We specialize in enchanting princess parties, mystical pirate adventures, and more magical themes.

  • Where are you located, and what areas do you serve?

    Pixies Parties is based in Calgary, AB, and we happily bring the magic to surrounding areas.

  • What characters do you offer for parties?

    Our delightful characters include Belle, Snow Queen, Snow Sister, Cinderella, Rapunzel, Ariel, Barbie, A Wizarding Student, and Penny the Pirate!

  • How much does a party package cost?

    Pricing varies based on the package and location, but you can find detailed pricing information on our website.

  • How long do your parties typically last?

    Party durations vary by package, but we'll make sure to provide you with a party that suits your needs.

  • What activities and games do you offer at your parties?

    We offer a range of whimsical activities, including storytime, themed games, and interactive adventures.

  • Do you provide costumes and accessories for the children?

    While children can dress up and immerse themselves in the fantasy, Pixies Parties does not provide costumes for the children to wear.

  • Are your entertainers trained and experienced with children?

    Our entertainers are not only skilled performers but also experienced in creating a magical experience for kids.

  • How far in advance should I book a party?

    We recommend booking your enchanting event at least 2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date.

  • Do you accommodate special requests or custom themes?

    We're happy to discuss customizations and special requests to make your child's dream party come true.

  • What COVID-19 safety measures do you have in place?

    Ensuring the safety and well-being of children is our priority. We follow all recommended guidelines for a safe and enchanting party experience. All of our performers are fully vaccinated.

  • What's your cancellation policy?

    We understand that life's surprises happen, and we'll do our best to accommodate changes. A $100 deposit is required at the time of booking. More information is available on the cancellation policy page

  • Can I add a second character to a package?

    As a sole performer, I'm currently only able to offer solo bookings. While I don't have the capacity to add a second character to the package, rest assured that I'll bring an abundance of magic and excitement to your child's party all on my own!

  • Do you do adult parties?

    Yes, absolutely! We offer enchanting entertainment for all ages, including adult parties. Whether you're planning a whimsical gathering for grown-ups or a magical event for the young at heart, we're here to sprinkle some pixie dust and make your celebration truly unforgettable!

  • What happens if the weather is bad on the day of the party?

    In the event of inclement weather, we'll work with you to find an alternative solution, whether it's rescheduling the party or moving it indoors.

  • Do you offer virtual or online party options?

    Yes, we offer virtual party experiences for those who prefer to celebrate from a distance. Contact us for more information on our virtual party packages.

  • Can you accommodate children with special needs or allergies?

    Yes, we strive to create inclusive and safe experiences for all children. Please let us know about any special requirements, and we'll do our best to accommodate them.

  • What age groups are your parties suitable for?

    Our parties are tailored for children aged 4 to 10, but we're happy to adapt activities to suit different age groups.

  • Can parents stay and watch the party?

    Absolutely! Parents are welcome to stay and join in the fun or observe from the sidelines, whichever they prefer.

  • Do you do outdoor events?

    Yes, we do outdoor events! However, please note that we don't offer refunds for inclement weather. You can reschedule for a $40 administration fee. We highly recommend having an indoor backup venue to ensure the magic can continue no matter the weather.

  • Can you do a character that is not listed on your website?

    At this time, we aren’t able to accommodate characters that aren’t listed on our website. We’ve put a lot of love and effort into perfecting our current character offerings to ensure each one delivers a truly magical experience. However, if there’s something special you’re looking for, feel free to share your ideas—we might be able to suggest some enchanting ways to make it work within our existing lineup!
