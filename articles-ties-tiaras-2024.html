Ties and Tiaras Ball

A Magical Family Event

June 18, 2024

Pixies Parties is delighted to announce the Ties and Tiaras Ball, a whimsical and enchanting event designed to create magical moments for families. The event will take place on June 29th, 2024, from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the Willow Ridge Community Centre in Calgary.


The Ties and Tiaras Ball promises an afternoon filled with fun and fantasy. Highlights of the event include:

  • Princess Parade: Little princes and princesses are invited to strut their stuff around the dance floor along with their favourite characters.
  • Family Dance Party: Show off your moves on the dance floor with your little ones!
  • Craft Station: Get creative and make memories together with delightful crafts!
  • Royal Photo Booth: Capture the magic with enchanting photos to cherish forever!
  • Face Painting by Face Art Calgary: Get ready for a magical transformation with stunning face painting designs that will delight guests of all ages. Whether your child wants to become a beautiful butterfly, a fierce tiger, or their favourite princess, Face Art Calgary will bring their dreams to life with creative and colourful face art.
Guests can also enjoy a variety of snacks and soft drinks available for purchase.
“We are thrilled to bring the Ties and Tiaras Ball to the families of Calgary,” said Erin Mackey, founder of Pixies Parties. “This event is all about creating unforgettable experiences and fostering joyful connections between parents and children. We look forward to seeing everyone dressed in their finest and ready to have a magical time.”

Tickets are priced at $20 each, and can be purchased online at Eventbrite.

For more information about the Ties and Tiaras Ball, please visit our event page at Eventbrite.

About Pixies Parties:
Pixies Parties specializes in children's party entertainment, bringing fairy-tale characters and magical moments to life. Based in Calgary, AB, Pixies Parties is dedicated to creating enchanting experiences that children and families will cherish forever.
