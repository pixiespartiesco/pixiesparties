<p>Pixies Parties is delighted to announce the Ties and Tiaras Ball, a whimsical and enchanting event designed to create magical moments for families. The event will take place on April 18, 2026 from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM at the Riverbend Community Centre in Calgary.<br/>
<br/>
<img src="/assets/img/events/ttb4.webp" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block col-12 col-md-6 col-lg-4"><br/>
The Ties and Tiaras Ball promises an afternoon filled with fun and fantasy. Highlights of the event include:<br/>
<br/>
<ul class="hanging-indent-list"><li><strong>Princess Parade:</strong> Little princes and princesses are invited to strut their stuff around the dance floor along with their favourite characters.</li>
<li><strong>Family Dance Party:</strong> Show off your moves on the dance floor with your little ones!</li>
<li><strong>Craft Station:</strong> Get creative and make memories together with delightful crafts!</li>
<li><strong>Royal Photo Booth:</strong> Capture the magic with enchanting photos to cherish forever!</li>
<li><strong>Face Painting by Face Art Calgary:</strong> Get ready for a magical transformation with stunning face painting designs that will delight guests of all ages. Whether your child wants to become a beautiful butterfly, a fierce tiger, or their favourite princess, Face Art Calgary will bring their dreams to life with creative and colourful face art.</li>
</ul>
Guests can also enjoy a variety of snacks and soft drinks available for purchase.
