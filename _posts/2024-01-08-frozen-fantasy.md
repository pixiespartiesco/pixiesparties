<h4>Frozen Fun: Activities Fit for Royalty</h4>
Snowflake Crafts:<br/>
Let creativity flurry with snowflake-making activities! Young artisans can create their own magical snowflakes to adorn the wintry kingdom.<br/>
<br/>
Frozen Sing-Along:<br/>
Embark on a musical journey with a Frozen sing-along session! Let the young royalty sing their hearts out to the beloved tunes of Arendelle.<br/>
<br/>
Freeze Dance:<br/>
Play the classic party game with a frosty twist. Dance to the top hits from the Frozen soundtrack, but whenever Elsa casts her frozen flurries and stops the music everyone turns into an ice statue.<br/>
<br/>
<h4>Frosty Feast: Delectable Delights for Snow Royals</h4>
Ice Queen's Banquet:<br/>
Craft a menu fit for the ice queen herself! Snowflake-shaped treats, frozen-themed desserts, and a stunning ice castle cake will be the crown jewels of the feast.<br/>
<br/>
Winter Buffet:<br/>
Create a feast table adorned with snowflake accents and frosty hues! Snowball cookies, hot cocoa stations, and sandwiches shaped like snowmen will delight the young partygoers.<br/>
