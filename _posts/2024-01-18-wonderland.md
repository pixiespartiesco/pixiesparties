<h4>
  Down the Rabbit Hole: Transforming Your Realm into Wonderland
</h4>Wonderland Decor Wonderland:<br>
Adorn your party kingdom with vibrant colors, oversized flowers, and whimsical characters that echo the enchanting
world of Wonderland.<br>
<br>
Mad Hatter's Chair:<br>
Craft a peculiar throne fit for the birthday VIP! An eccentric chair adorned with mismatched teacups, playing
cards, and a whimsical hat will be the perfect seat for the day's honoree.<br>

<h4>
  Curious Costumes: Embracing Wonderland Whimsy
</h4>Wonderland Attire:<br>
Encourage young guests to don their most whimsical costumes! From the Cheshire Cat's grin to the Queen of Hearts'
crown, let their imagination run wild with Wonderland-inspired outfits.<br>
<br>
Wonderland Characters' Visit:<br>
Summon real-life Wonderland characters to join the celebrations! At Pixies Parties, our charming characters are
ready to bring the magic and madness of Wonderland to life.<br>
<br>	

<h4>
  Tea Party Tidbits: Delightful Delicacies for Wonderland Tummies
</h4>Mad Hatter's Tea Menu:<br>
Craft a menu fit for the Mad Hatter's tea party! Mini sandwiches, whimsical cookies, and a fantastical cake will be
the talk of the tea table.<br>
<br>
Wonderland Buffet:<br>
Create a buffet table adorned with teapots, top hats, and Wonderland trinkets! Colourful snacks, themed treats, and
a variety of iced teas will satisfy even the most curious appetites.<br>

<h4>
  Wonderland Whimsies: Activities and Games Fit for Little Dreamers
</h4>Queen's Croquet Challenge:<br>
Organize a lively game of croquet with a Wonderland twist! Let the young ones play the Queen's croquet challenge
with inflatable flamingos and hedgehogs.<br>
<br>
Hat Decorating:<br>
Let creativity bloom with a hat-decorating activity! Young dreamers can design their own Wonderland-inspired hats
to wear during the festivities.<br>
<br>
Mad Hatter's Tea Party:<br>
Host a Mad Hatter's tea party where young guests can enjoy tea party games like musical chairs with a whimsical
twist! Set up a table with tea cups, saucers, and teapots, and play lively music as they move around the table.<br>
<br>
<h4>
  Picture-Perfect Moments: Capturing the Whimsy of Wonderland
</h4>Wonderland Photo Corner:<br>
Create a picturesque photo area with oversized flowers, teacup props, and Wonderland backdrops! Let young guests
capture their enchanting transformations amidst the whimsy.<br>
<br>
Wonderland Keepsakes:<br>
Bid adieu with whimsical favors! Playing cards, Wonderland-themed trinkets, or mini tea sets will be treasured
reminders of the magical day.<br>
