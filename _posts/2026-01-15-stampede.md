# Beyond the Flapjack

## How to Keep Kids Entertained at Your Corporate Stampede Breakfast

The Calgary Stampede is the crown jewel of our city’s corporate calendar. For Social Committees, the "Pancake Breakfast" is a tradition that builds community, boosts morale, and shows appreciation for employees' families. However, there is a distinct challenge every organizer faces: how do you keep the kids engaged while the adults network over coffee and sausages?

To transform a standard breakfast into a legendary corporate event, the secret lies in balancing high-energy "Western" activities with professional character-led structure.

![Rapunzel](/assets/img/characters/rapunzelstampede.webp "Rapunzel")

## Why Your Stampede Breakfast Needs a "Royal Cowboy" Touch

It might seem like a contrast—a fairy-tale princess at a rodeo event—but character entertainers like Rapunzel (with a western twist!) provide something that bouncy castles and haybale seating cannot: focused engagement.

While the Stampede atmosphere is high-energy, it can also be overwhelming for younger children. A professional entertainer acts as a "magic anchor." They don’t just stand there for photos; they manage the flow of the "Kid Zone," ensuring that even the shyest child feels included in the festivities.

## The Ultimate Stampede Activity List for Kids

To create a truly immersive experience, we recommend a mix of self-led stations and facilitated games. Here are the top activities that keep mini-buckaroos entertained:

- Roping the "Steel Bull": Set up a stationary wooden or plastic bull head on a hay bale. Provide child-sized lassos and have your entertainer teach the kids the "roping basics."
- The Mobile Petting Zoo: A perennial favorite. Interacting with goats, bunnies, and lambs provides a tactile, calm experience amidst the loud country music.
- Panning for "Gold": Fill a galvanized steel trough with sand, water, and spray-painted gold pebbles. Give the kids sifting pans for a historical Calgary thrill.
- Horseshoe Toss: A safe, rubber version of the classic game. It’s competitive, easy to understand, and great for hand-eye coordination.
- Mini-Line Dancing Lessons: Have your character entertainer lead a "Lil’ Stompers" dance class. It gets the kids moving and creates a fantastic photo op for parents.
- Tinsel & Western Face Painting: While the kids wait for their pancakes, a station for "glitter beards" or cheek-art (think horseshoes and sheriff stars) keeps them occupied.

## The "Rapunzel" Advantage for Social Committees

Why hire a character for a Stampede event? It’s about crowd control and brand image. 
1.  Stress-Free Supervision: When a character like Rapunzel leads a storytime or a game of "Simon Says: Cowboy Edition," the parents are free to actually enjoy their breakfast and talk to their colleagues.
2.  Professionalism: Corporate events require reliability. Professional entertainers arrive on time, stay in character regardless of the heat, and are experts at managing large groups of children (often up to 20 or 30 at a time).
3.  Memorable Branding: Employees will remember the year "the company brought in a princess for the kids." It adds a layer of "above and beyond" effort that reflects well on the Social Committee’s planning.
