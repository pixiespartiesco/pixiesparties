## 🥞 Why Book a Character for Your Stampede Event?

Stampede events are high-energy, bustling, and full of community spirit—but they can also involve long lines and waiting times, especially for little ones. Here is how our characters elevate your celebration:  

**Line Entertainment & Crowd Management:** Long queue for pancakes or the brisket buffet? Our characters excel at walking the lines, chatting with waiting families, and keeping children completely engaged and entertained while they wait.  
**Iconic Photo Opportunities:** Give your guests a unique keepsake! Parents love capturing the moment their favorite character sports a custom western hat and stampede style next to the classic red *Calgary Stampede* tents.  
**Community Engagement:** From corporate staff parties to massive community association breakfasts, our characters bring an instant festive, family-friendly atmosphere that makes your organization stand out.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q: Can characters attend outdoor events?
**A:** Yes, absolutely! Our Stampede outfits are designed specifically for outdoor summer celebrations. However, to keep the costumes pristine and ensure performer safety, we do require a shaded area or tent coverage if the temperature exceeds 30°C or in the event of rain.

#### Q: Do you travel outside of Calgary proper?
**A:** Yes. We serve Calgary and surrounding areas (including Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, and Chestermere). Events outside Calgary city limits may be subject to a modest travel fee.

#### Q: Can we request specific characters for the Stampede theme?
**A:** Yes! Our Rapunzel-inspired look is our signature Stampede favorite, but we can adapt several of our popular characters with a western flair upon request. Contact us early to discuss your vision!
