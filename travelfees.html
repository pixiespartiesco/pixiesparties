Travel Fees

At Pixies Parties, we love bringing magic and merriment to as many children as possible!
If you're planning a party outside of Calgary, please note that there are travel fees to cover our journey from our starting location in South Calgary.
Our horses need a few more carrots and oats to pull our magical carriage further, so these travel fees help keep our horses happy and well-fed!

Travel Fee Breakdown

  • $0.40 per kilometre for the distance travelled.
  • $15 per hour for travel time.
