Whisked Away into Royalty: Transforming Your Realm into a Palace of Elegance

Royal Tea Party Decor:
Adorn your party kingdom with pastel hues, lace doilies, and blossoming flowers, creating an ambiance fit for princesses. Elegant tea sets, glistening tiaras, and dainty table settings will set the stage for a regal affair.

Princess Throne:
Craft a throne fit for the birthday royalty! A plush chair draped in satin, adorned with flowers, and a sparkling tiara will ensure a regal seat for the day's princess.

Elegant Ensembles: Embracing Royal Fashion

Princess Dress Code:
Encourage young princesses to don their most elegant gowns! Tiara-topped hairstyles, flowing dresses, and touches of lace will transform them into the belle of the royal tea.

Summon a real-life princess to join the tea festivities! At Pixies Parties, our enchanting princesses are ready to captivate young hearts with grace, warmth, and tales from their magical realms.

Tea-Time Tidbits: Delectable Delights for Royal Palates

Princess Tea Menu:
Craft a menu befitting a royal tea party! Delicate finger sandwiches, petite pastries, and a crown-worthy cake will be the crowning glory of the tea-time feast.

Tea Party Buffet:
Create a picturesque buffet adorned with teacups, flowers, and dainty treats! Fruit skewers, heart-shaped cookies, and a variety of teas will delight the young princesses' taste buds.

Regal Revelries: Activities and Games for Princess Pleasure

Tiara Decorating:
Let creativity sparkle with a tiara-decorating activity! Young princesses can bedazzle their own crowns with gems, pearls, and sparkles.

Storybook Moment:
Embark on a journey of enchantment with a princess storybook reading! Gather 'round as the princess regales the young royals with tales of bravery and magic.

Cherished Keepsakes: Capturing Princess Memories

Princess Party Favors:
